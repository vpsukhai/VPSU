About Us

Discover our legacy of educational excellence since 1989

A Legacy of Excellence in Education

Veena Public School was established on 10th February 1989 with the vision of providing quality education to students in and around Ukhai, Siwan. Our esteemed founder, Mr. Umashankar Sah (MA), laid the foundation of an institution committed to academic excellence, strong moral values, and the holistic development of every child.

For over 35 years, Veena Public School has nurtured young minds from Nursery to Class 8, adhering to the CBSE curriculum. With a team of 15 qualified teachers and 3 dedicated non-teaching staff, we focus on delivering personalized and inclusive education in a caring environment.

We are strong believers in experiential learning and strive to go beyond textbooks. The school actively conducts yoga sessions, sports activities, quiz competitions, and cultural programs that help shape students' character, health, and creativity.

Our school is registered under the Government of Bihar and proudly complies with the RTE Act 2009, ensuring 25% reservation in Class 1 for children from economically weaker and disadvantaged backgrounds.

Veena Public School continues to evolve with the changing times, aiming to inspire a new generation of learners who are confident, capable, and compassionate.
