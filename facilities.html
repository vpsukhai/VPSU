Our Facilities

Creating an environment for holistic learning and growth

Learning Environment at Veena Public School

At Veena Public School, we take pride in being a model village school that combines affordability with quality education. Our facilities are designed to support the needs of rural students while aligning with the CBSE curriculum and modern learning standards.

Our aim is to create an environment where children feel safe, inspired, and empowered to learn and grow.
