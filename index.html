Welcome to Veena Public School

Founded on 10th February 1989 by Mr. Umashankar Sah (M.A), Veena Public School has been a beacon of quality education in Ukhai, Siwan. Managed by Veena Educational and Technical Welfare Society, our school provides a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence and personal growth.

We follow the CBSE pattern curriculum offering education from Nursery to Class 8, with instruction in Hindi, English, Sanskrit, and Urdu. Our dedicated team of 15 teachers and 3 non-teaching staff work tirelessly to ensure our students receive the best possible education.
